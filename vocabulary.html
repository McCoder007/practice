New Words

Day 16

action movie

动作片
noun

I love action movies because they're fast and exciting.

我喜欢动作片，因为它们又快又刺激。

aliens

外星人
noun

In the movie, aliens attack Earth.

在电影中，外星人攻击地球。

animated movie

动画片
noun

My favorite animated movie is Finding Nemo — the cartoon characters are so cute!

我最喜欢的动画片是《海底总动员》——卡通人物太可爱了！

cartoon characters

卡通人物
noun

Mickey Mouse is a famous cartoon character.

米老鼠是一个著名的卡通人物。

comedy

喜剧片
noun

We watched a comedy last night and laughed the whole time.

我们昨晚看了一部喜剧片，笑个不停。

costumes

服装
noun

The costumes in the musical were colorful and traditional.

音乐剧中的服装五颜六色又很传统。

fall in love

相爱 / 爱上
verb

They fall in love while traveling in Europe.

他们在欧洲旅行时相爱了。

fight scenes

打斗场面
noun

The fight scenes in that kung-fu movie were intense.

那部功夫电影的打斗场面很激烈。

hilarious

非常好笑的
adjective

That comedy was hilarious—I laughed so hard.

那部喜剧太好笑了——我笑得停不下来。

horror movie

恐怖片
noun

I can't sleep after watching horror movies — they're too scary for me!

看完恐怖片我睡不着——对我来说太吓人了！

love story

爱情故事
noun

Titanic is a famous love story.

《泰坦尼克号》是一部著名的爱情故事。

monster

怪物
noun

A huge monster appears from the forest.

一只巨大的怪物从森林中出现。

musical

音乐剧电影
noun

The musical had great costumes and amazing songs!

这部音乐剧电影的服装和歌曲都非常棒！

scary

吓人的
adjective

That horror movie was really scary!

那部恐怖电影真的很吓人！

science fiction (sci-fi)

科幻片
noun

Sci-fi movies make me imagine what the future could be like.

科幻片让我想象未来会是什么样子。

special effects

特效
noun

The movie had great special effects with realistic explosions.

电影的特效很棒，爆炸场面很逼真。

stunts

特技
noun

The car stunts in the movie were amazing.

电影里的汽车特技非常精彩。

subtitled

有字幕的
adjective

It was a Japanese film but subtitled in English.

那是一部日语电影，但有英文字幕。

suspense

悬念
noun

The suspense in the spy movie kept me on the edge of my seat.

那部间谍片的悬念让我一直很紧张。

takes place

发生在
verb

The story takes place in 19th-century Paris.

故事发生在19世纪的巴黎。

thriller

惊悚片
noun

Thrillers always keep me guessing what will happen next.

惊悚片总是让我猜不到接下来会发生什么。

war movie

战争片
noun

War movies help me understand what soldiers go through.

战争片让我了解士兵们经历了什么。
