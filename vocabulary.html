New Words

Day 13

below

在下面
preposition

The shoes are below the bed.

鞋子在床下面。

blunt

直率的 / 钝的
adjective

He was blunt and said the truth.

他很直率，说了实话。

borrowing

借用
verb

She is borrowing my book.

她在借我的书。

bridge

桥
noun

We walked across the bridge.

我们走过了桥。

calm

平静的
adjective

She stayed calm during the storm.

她在暴风雨中保持冷静。

collect

收集
verb

I collect stamps from many countries.

我收集来自很多国家的邮票。

common

常见的 / 普通的
adjective

Apples are a common fruit.

苹果是常见的水果。

cottonwood

杨树 / 棉白杨
noun

The cottonwood trees grow near the river.

棉白杨长在河边。

dead end

死胡同 / 尽头
noun

We turned around at the dead end.

我们在死胡同掉头了。

desire

渴望 / 欲望
noun

She has a strong desire to learn.

她有强烈的学习欲望。

fire hydrant

消防栓
noun

The fire truck stopped by the fire hydrant.

消防车停在消防栓旁边。

goal

目标
noun

My goal is to pass the exam.

我的目标是通过考试。

greasy

油腻的
adjective

These fries are too greasy.

这些薯条太油腻了。

immediately

立刻
adverb

Come here immediately!

立刻过来！

information

信息 / 资料
noun

I need more information about the trip.

我需要更多关于旅行的信息。

invitation

邀请
noun

I got an invitation to the party.

我收到了一张派对的邀请函。

Japanese maple

日本枫树
noun

The Japanese maple has red leaves.

日本枫树有红色的叶子。

lonely

孤独的
adjective

He felt lonely at night.

他晚上感到孤单。

luggage

行李
noun

I packed my luggage for the trip.

我为旅行打包了行李。

notice

注意到
verb

I didn't notice the sign.

我没注意到那个标志。

plain

普通的 / 朴素的
adjective

She wore a plain white shirt.

她穿着一件朴素的白衬衫。

protection

保护
noun

Sunscreen gives your skin protection.

防晒霜可以保护你的皮肤。

sensitive

敏感的
adjective

He is very sensitive to noise.

他对噪音很敏感。

siren

警报器
noun

The ambulance has a loud siren.

救护车有响亮的警报器。

strange

奇怪的
adjective

That sound is very strange.

那个声音很奇怪。

stretch

伸展
verb

I stretch every morning.

我每天早上伸展身体。

water slide

滑水道
noun

The water slide is fun at the pool.

游泳池的滑水道很好玩。
