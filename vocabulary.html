New Words

Day 12

Apartment

公寓
noun

I live in a small apartment.

我住在一个小公寓里。

Appointment

预约 / 约会（正式）
noun

I have a doctor's appointment today.

我今天有个看医生的预约。

Crackers

饼干（咸的）
noun

I ate cheese and crackers.

我吃了奶酪和咸饼干。

Freeway

高速公路
noun

The cars move fast on the freeway.

高速公路上的车开得很快。

Guitar

吉他
noun

He plays the guitar very well.

他吉他弹得很好。

I almost fell

我差点摔倒了
sentence

I almost fell on the stairs.

我在楼梯上差点摔倒。

Motivated

有动力的
adjective

She is very motivated to learn English.

她学英语很有动力。

Sleepy

困的
adjective

I feel sleepy after lunch.

午饭后我觉得很困。

Special

特别的
adjective

Today is a special day.

今天是个特别的日子。

Station

车站
noun

The train leaves from this station.

火车从这个车站出发。

Tow truck

拖车（救援车）
noun

A tow truck came to help.

拖车来了帮忙。

Trouble

麻烦 / 困难
noun

He is in big trouble.

他遇到大麻烦了。

Tunnel

隧道
noun

The bus went through a tunnel.

公交车穿过了一个隧道。

Wolf

狼
noun

A wolf lives in the forest.

森林里住着一只狼。

Wolves

狼（复数）
noun

Wolves hunt in groups.

狼成群结队地狩猎。

Youth

青年 / 年轻人
noun

The youth are full of energy.

年轻人充满活力。
