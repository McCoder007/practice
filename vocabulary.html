New Words

Day 1

attend

参加
verb

Will you attend the meeting tomorrow?

你明天会参加会议吗？

automatically

自动地
adverb

The lights turn on automatically when someone enters the room.

当有人进入房间时，灯会自动打开。

balance

平衡
noun

It's important to maintain a balance between work and life.

保持工作和生活之间的平衡很重要。

balancing

保持平衡
verb

She is good at balancing her career and family responsibilities.

她善于平衡事业和家庭责任。

dressed formal

穿着正式
phrase

For the wedding, everyone should be dressed formal.

参加婚礼时，每个人都应该穿正式的服装。

dressed informal

穿着随便
phrase

The party is casual, so you can come dressed informal.

聚会是休闲的，所以你可以穿着休闲。

event

活动
noun

We're organizing a special event for the holiday.

我们正在为假期组织一个特别活动。

honk

按喇叭
verb

Please don't honk your horn in residential areas.

请不要在住宅区按喇叭。

horn

喇叭
noun

The driver honked his horn at the pedestrian.

司机对行人按喇叭。

hurry

赶紧
verb

We need to hurry or we'll miss the train.

我们需要快点，否则我们会错过火车。

impatient

不耐烦的
adjective

He gets impatient when he has to wait in line.

当他必须排队等候时，他会变得不耐烦。

interrupt

打断
verb

It's rude to interrupt when someone is talking.

当某人说话时打断他是不礼貌的。

patient

有耐心的
adjective

My teacher is very patient with students who learn slowly.

我的老师对学习慢的学生很有耐心。

people

人们
noun

There are many people in the shopping mall today.

今天购物中心里有很多人。

person

人
noun

There's a person waiting outside for you.

有一个人在外面等你。

polite

有礼貌的
adjective

Remember to be polite when meeting new people.

记得在遇到新朋友时要有礼貌。

quickly

快速地
adverb

She walks quickly to catch the bus.

她快速地走路去赶公交车。

recklessly

鲁莽地
adverb

He drives recklessly on the highway.

他在高速公路上开车鲁莽。

sport

运动
noun

Soccer is the most popular sport in many countries.

足球是许多国家最受欢迎的运动。
