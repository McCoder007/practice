New Words | 生词

Day 19

Cheek

脸颊
noun

She kissed him on the cheek.

她亲了他的脸颊一下。

Civil rights

公民权利
noun

Martin Luther King fought for civil rights.

马丁·路德·金为公民权利而奋斗。

Deranged

精神错乱的
adjective

The man in the movie was completely deranged.

电影里那个人完全精神错乱了。

Disability

残疾
noun

He has a disability but plays sports well.

他有残疾但运动很厉害。

Discrimination

歧视
noun

Discrimination is wrong and unfair.

歧视是不对的，也是不公平的。

Employment

就业
noun

She is looking for employment in the city.

她正在城市里找工作。

Fluent

流利的
adjective

He is fluent in English and Japanese.

他英语和日语都很流利。

Forehead

额头
noun

He has sweat on his forehead.

他的额头上有汗。

Measure

测量
verb

I will measure the table before buying a cloth.

我会先测量桌子再买桌布。

Native speaker

母语者
noun

She is a native speaker of Spanish.

她是西班牙语的母语者。

Odd

奇怪的
adjective

That movie was very odd.

那部电影很奇怪。

Protesters

抗议者
noun

The protesters held signs in the street.

抗议者在街上举着标语牌。

Religion

宗教
noun

People have different kinds of religion.

人们信仰不同的宗教。

Solid

固体的 / 坚固的
adjective

Ice is the solid form of water.

冰是水的固体形式。

Successful

成功的
adjective

She is a successful businesswoman.

她是一个成功的女商人。
