New Words

Day 14

ad

广告
noun

I saw a shoe ad on YouTube.

我在 YouTube 上看到一个鞋子的广告。

antivirus software

杀毒软件
noun

I installed antivirus software on my computer.

我在电脑上安装了杀毒软件。

carpet

地毯
noun

The carpet is soft and warm.

地毯又软又暖和。

crave

渴望
verb

I crave my boyfriend's kiss every day.

我每天都渴望男朋友的吻。

deer

鹿
noun

A deer ran across the road.

一只鹿跑过马路。

electronic

电子的
adjective

This is an electronic toy.

这是一个电子玩具。

facts

事实
noun

We need facts to understand the news.

我们需要事实来理解新闻。

flip flops

人字拖
noun

I should not wear flip flops when I go for a walk at night.

晚上散步时我不应该穿人字拖。

freezing up

卡住 / 冻住（技术类故障）
verb

My computer keeps freezing up.

我的电脑一直卡住。

freezing

冰冷的 / 冻的
adjective

It's freezing outside today.

今天外面冷得要命。

hair tie

发圈 / 发绳
noun

I lost my hair tie.

我的发圈丢了。

humans

人类
noun

Humans need food and water to live.

人类需要食物和水才能生存。

oatmeal

燕麦粥
noun

I eat oatmeal for breakfast.

我早餐吃燕麦粥。

power cord

电源线
noun

I can't find the power cord for my laptop.

我找不到笔记本电脑的电源线。

sandals

凉鞋
noun

I wear sandals in summer.

我夏天穿凉鞋。

swollen

肿胀的
adjective

My ankle is swollen.

我的脚踝肿了。

toast

吐司 / 烤面包
noun

I had toast and eggs this morning.

我今天早上吃了吐司和鸡蛋。

website

网站
noun

I found the recipe on a website.

我在一个网站上找到了这个食谱。

wireless connection

无线连接
noun

The wireless connection on my deck is weak.

我阳台上的无线网络信号很弱。

wiping your body

擦身体
verb

After a shower, I dry off by wiping my body.

洗澡后我擦干身体。

worm

蠕虫 / 小虫子
noun

A worm came out of the soil.

一条虫子从土里爬出来了。
