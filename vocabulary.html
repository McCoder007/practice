New Words

Day 17

accomplish

完成
verb

I want to accomplish all my goals this year.

我想在今年完成我所有的目标。

aim

目标
verb

I aim to finish my homework before dinner.

我计划在晚饭前完成作业。

ambition

雄心
noun

She has the ambition to become a doctor.

她有成为医生的雄心。

goal

目标
noun

My goal is to read 10 books this month.

我的目标是这个月读十本书。

improve

提高
verb

I practice every day to improve my English.

我每天练习以提高我的英语。

motivate

激励
verb

My teacher always motivates me to study hard.

我的老师总是激励我努力学习。

plan

计划
noun

We need a plan to finish this project.

我们需要一个计划来完成这个项目。

progress

进步
noun

I'm making good progress in math class.

我在数学课上取得了很大的进步。

success

成功
noun

Hard work is the key to success.

努力是成功的关键。

target

目标
noun

My target is to run 5 kilometers this week.

我的目标是这周跑五公里。
