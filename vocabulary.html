New Words

Day 15

Put them away

把它们收起来
verb phrase

After using the ear buds, I put them away.

用完耳机后，我把它们收起来了。

Hook it up

把它连接起来
verb phrase

He wants to hook it up to the big screen.

他想把它连接到大屏幕上。

Look it up

查一下它
verb phrase

If you don't know the word, just look it up.

如果你不认识这个词，就查一下它。

Pick it up

捡起来它
verb phrase

I dropped my phone and had to pick it up.

我的手机掉了，我得把它捡起来。

Put it down

把它放下
verb phrase

Please put it down on the table.

请把它放在桌子上。

Print it out

把它打印出来
verb phrase

I need this document, so I'll print it out.

我需要这份文件，所以我会把它打印出来。

Put them in

把它们放进去
verb phrase

These batteries are new, let me put them in.

这些电池是新的，我来把它们装进去。

Take it apart

拆开它
verb phrase

He tried to fix the printer, so he had to take it apart.

他想修打印机，所以他必须拆开它。

Throw it away

把它扔掉
verb phrase

This charger is broken—I'll throw it away.

这个充电器坏了——我把它扔掉。

Turn it down

把音量调小
verb phrase

The music is too loud. Can you turn it down?

音乐太吵了。你能把音量调小吗？

Turn it up

把音量调大
verb phrase

I can't hear the video. Let me turn it up.

我听不清视频。我来把音量调大。

Plug it in

插上电源
verb phrase

I don't know how to plug it in.

我不知道怎么把它插上电源。
