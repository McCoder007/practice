New Words

Day 10

Bread

面包
noun

I eat bread for breakfast.

我早餐吃面包。

Broccoli

西兰花
noun

Broccoli is a healthy vegetable.

西兰花是一种健康的蔬菜。

Calories

卡路里
noun

This drink has a lot of calories.

这杯饮料有很多卡路里。

Carrot

胡萝卜
noun

Rabbits like to eat carrots.

兔子喜欢吃胡萝卜。

Disagree

不同意
verb

I disagree with your idea.

我不同意你的想法。

Freezer

冷冻室
noun

The ice cream is in the freezer.

冰淇淋在冷冻室里。

Ketchup bottle

番茄酱瓶
noun

Pass me the ketchup bottle, please.

请把番茄酱瓶递给我。

Leftovers

剩饭菜
noun

We ate leftovers for lunch.

我们午餐吃了剩饭菜。

Loaf of bread

一条面包
noun phrase

I bought one loaf of bread.

我买了一条面包。

Loaves of bread

多条面包
noun phrase

She bought three loaves of bread.

她买了三条面包。

Mango

芒果
noun

Mango is my favorite fruit.

芒果是我最喜欢的水果。

Margarine

人造黄油
noun

I spread margarine on my toast.

我在吐司上抹了人造黄油。

Milk

牛奶
noun

I drink milk every day.

我每天喝牛奶。

Mustard

芥末酱
noun

I don't like mustard on my sandwich.

我不喜欢在三明治里放芥末酱。

Percent

百分比
noun

The sign says 50 percent off.

标牌上写着五折。

Peppers

辣椒 / 彩椒
noun

She is cutting red and green peppers.

她在切红椒和青椒。

Pineapple

菠萝
noun

Pineapple is sweet and juicy.

菠萝又甜又多汁。

Pound

磅（重量单位）
noun

The apples weigh two pounds.

这些苹果有两磅重。

Refrigerator

冰箱
noun

The milk is in the refrigerator.

牛奶在冰箱里。

Slice

薄片 / 切片
noun/verb

I ate a slice of pizza.

我吃了一片比萨。

Soda

汽水
noun

He drank a can of soda.

他喝了一罐汽水。

Soy sauce

酱油
noun

I added soy sauce to the rice.

我在米饭里加了酱油。

Tomato

番茄
noun

I put a tomato in my salad.

我在沙拉里放了一个番茄。

Treat

奖励（好吃的东西）/ 请客
noun/verb

Ice cream is a nice treat.

冰淇淋是一种美味的奖励。

Vegetables

蔬菜
noun

You should eat more vegetables.

你应该多吃蔬菜。
