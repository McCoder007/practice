New Words | 生词

Day 21

Central Park

中央公园
proper noun

When I visited New York City, I walked around Central Park.

我去纽约市时，在中央公园散步。

Disneyland

迪士尼乐园
proper noun

Families visit Disneyland in California for fun rides.

家庭会去加州的迪士尼乐园玩游乐设施。

Empire State Building

帝国大厦
proper noun

From the Empire State Building in New York, you can see the whole city.

在纽约的帝国大厦上，你可以看到整个城市。

Golden Gate Bridge

金门大桥
proper noun

People take photos on the Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco.

人们在旧金山的金门大桥上拍照。

Grand Canyon

大峡谷
proper noun

Tourists hike at the Grand Canyon in Arizona.

游客在亚利桑那州的大峡谷徒步旅行。

Harvard University

哈佛大学
proper noun

Many students dream of studying at Harvard University in Massachusetts.

许多学生梦想在马萨诸塞州的哈佛大学学习。

Lake Tahoe

太浩湖
proper noun

In California and Nevada, people swim and ski at Lake Tahoe.

在加州和内华达州，人们在太浩湖游泳和滑雪。

Mount Rainier

雷尼尔山
proper noun

Near Seattle, many people hike around Mount Rainier.

在西雅图附近，很多人去雷尼尔山徒步。

Olympic National Park

奥林匹克国家公园
proper noun

In Washington State, Olympic National Park has forests and beaches.

在华盛顿州，奥林匹克国家公园有森林和海滩。

Palm Springs

棕榈泉
proper noun

In Southern California, Palm Springs is very hot in summer.

在南加州，棕榈泉夏天非常热。

Pike Place Market

派克市场
proper noun

In Seattle, people watch fish fly at Pike Place Market.

在西雅图，人们在派克市场看鱼被扔来扔去。

San Diego Zoo

圣地亚哥动物园
proper noun

Families visit the San Diego Zoo in Southern California to see pandas.

家庭去南加州的圣地亚哥动物园看熊猫。

Santa Monica Pier

圣莫尼卡码头
proper noun

At Santa Monica Pier in Los Angeles, people ride the Ferris wheel.

在洛杉矶的圣莫尼卡码头，人们坐摩天轮。

Snoqualmie Falls

斯诺夸尔米瀑布
proper noun

Near Seattle, many visitors take photos at Snoqualmie Falls.

在西雅图附近，很多游客在斯诺夸尔米瀑布拍照。

Space Needle

太空针塔
proper noun

When you visit Seattle, you can go up the Space Needle.

去西雅图时，你可以登上太空针塔。

Stanford University

斯坦福大学
proper noun

In California, many students study at Stanford University.

在加州，许多学生在斯坦福大学学习。

Statue of Liberty

自由女神像
proper noun

Visitors take a boat to see the Statue of Liberty in New York Harbor.

游客坐船去纽约港看自由女神像。

Times Square

时代广场
proper noun

At night in New York City, Times Square is full of bright lights.

在纽约市的晚上，时代广场灯火通明。

University of Washington

华盛顿大学
proper noun

In Seattle, students take photos of cherry blossoms at the University of Washington.

在西雅图，学生在华盛顿大学拍樱花照片。

Walt Disney World

华特迪士尼世界
proper noun

In Florida, families spend many days at Walt Disney World.

在佛罗里达，家庭会在华特迪士尼世界玩很多天。
