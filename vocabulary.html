New Words

Day 11

Abusive

虐待的 / 辱骂的
adjective

He left because of the abusive behavior.

他因为受到虐待行为而离开了。

Asparagus

芦笋
noun

I ate asparagus with my dinner.

我晚餐吃了芦笋。

Battery

电池
noun

My battery is full now.

我的电池现在是满的。

Blind

失明的 / 看不见的
adjective

The blind man uses a cane.

这个盲人用手杖走路。

Cotton candy

棉花糖
noun

I bought cotton candy at the fair.

我在游乐场买了棉花糖。

Definitely

绝对地 / 肯定
adverb

I definitely want to go.

我一定想去。

Discuss

讨论
verb

Let's discuss the homework.

我们来讨论一下作业吧。

Exam

考试
noun

I have a math exam tomorrow.

我明天有一场数学考试。

Enough

足够的
adjective

I have enough water.

我有足够的水。

Fried food

油炸食物
noun phrase

Fried food tastes good but is not healthy.

油炸食物很好吃但不健康。

Frustrating

令人沮丧的
adjective

Waiting too long is frustrating.

等太久真的很令人沮丧。

Grill

烤架 / 烧烤
noun/verb

We grill chicken outside.

我们在外面烤鸡肉。

Grill food

烤食物
verb phrase

I like to grill food on the weekend.

我喜欢在周末烤食物。

Mood

心情
noun

She is in a good mood today.

她今天心情很好。

Moon

月亮
noun

The moon is very bright tonight.

今晚的月亮很亮。

My phone battery is low

我的手机电量很低
sentence

My phone battery is low.

我的手机电量很低。

Probably

可能 / 大概
adverb

I will probably go to the party.

我可能会去参加派对。

Properly

正确地 / 恰当地
adverb

Please wash your hands properly.

请正确地洗手。

Recycle

回收利用
verb

We recycle plastic bottles.

我们回收塑料瓶。

Sidewalk chalk

人行道粉笔
noun phrase

The kids are drawing with sidewalk chalk.

孩子们在用人行道粉笔画画。

Slice

薄片 / 切片
noun/verb

I ate a slice of cake.

我吃了一片蛋糕。

Steam

蒸 / 蒸汽
verb/noun

The rice is hot with steam.

米饭很热，有蒸汽。

Steam food

蒸食物
verb phrase

My mom likes to steam food.

我妈妈喜欢蒸食物。

Stomachache

肚子疼
noun

I have a stomachache after eating too much.

我吃太多后肚子疼。

Summer break

暑假
noun

We go to the beach during summer break.

我们暑假去海边玩。

Too much

太多
phrase

I ate too much candy.

我吃了太多糖。

Trivial

不重要的 / 琐碎的
adjective

That's a trivial problem.

那是个小问题。

Typing

打字
noun

I am practicing typing.

我在练习打字。
